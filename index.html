FULL PROFILE

Laurentius Kevin Hendinata is an engineer specializing in building physics and sustainable energy, focusing on acoustic performance of building openings. He holds a Bachelor of Engineering from Universitas Gadjah Mada and is currently pursuing a master's degree in acoustic engineering through the Erasmus Mundus WAVES program.

Kevin is open to collaboration opportunities in architectural acoustics and building physics. Contact: kevinhendinata@outlook.com

Decoupled CLT Junctions' Insulation Parameters

This project focused on quantifying Kij parameters for CLT junctions to control flanking sound transmission in timber buildings.

Key aspects included:

  • Finite element modeling in COMSOL
  • Experimental validation using laser Doppler vibrometry
  • Statistical energy analysis for sound transmission loss
