Acoustic Analysis for Green Mosque Dzarratul Muthmainnah

This comprehensive acoustic consultation was conducted for the renovation of Dzarratul Muthmainnah Green Mosque in South Tangerang. The project focused on optimizing speech intelligibility and worship experience through systematic evaluation of room acoustics and sound system performance.

Detailed measurements of reverberation time, speech transmission index, and background noise levels were performed. Computational modeling was then utilized to simulate various design scenarios, leading to evidence-based recommendations for acoustic treatment placement and sound system configuration.

The project outcomes have been implemented in the mosque's renovation, serving as a model for integrating acoustic considerations into religious architecture in tropical climates.
