Acoustic Analysis and Recommendation for Green Mosque Dzarratul Muthmainnah in South Tangerang

This project involved conducting an acoustic analysis for the renovation of the Dzarratul Muthmainnah Green Mosque. The focus was on optimizing the sound system and improving room acoustics to enhance the worship experience.

We assessed the existing conditions and provided recommendations for sound system upgrades, including speaker placement and acoustic treatment options.

The project aimed to create a serene and acoustically pleasant environment for the congregation, ensuring that sound quality is maintained during prayers and events.

Through this project, we demonstrated the importance of acoustics in religious spaces and how it can significantly impact the overall experience of worshippers.

Parametric Study on Transmission Loss of A Plenum Windows: A Geometrical Analysis

This research focused on evaluating the transmission and insertion loss of plenum windows using advanced simulation techniques.

We conducted a parametric study with variations in glass separation, overlapping, pane angle, and the addition of sonic crystals to optimize performance.

The findings provide valuable insights into the design of energy-efficient windows that can significantly reduce noise pollution while maintaining aesthetic appeal.

This project contributes to the development of innovative building materials that enhance both comfort and sustainability in urban environments.

Transparent Wood as Building Material and Passive Cooling Component

This project explores the potential of transparent wood as a revolutionary building material that combines aesthetics with functionality.

We investigated its properties and applications in passive cooling systems, aiming to reduce energy consumption in buildings.

The research highlights the benefits of using transparent wood in architecture, including its ability to provide natural light while maintaining thermal comfort.

Our findings suggest that transparent wood could play a significant role in the future of sustainable building practices.

Development of Baseline and Roadmap for Energy Efficiency in Buildings Across Indonesia

This project involved surveying and modeling baseline energy consumption in various cities across Indonesia.

We formulated energy efficiency measures aimed at achieving significant energy savings and reducing carbon footprints.

The roadmap developed serves as a guideline for policymakers and stakeholders to implement effective energy-saving strategies in the building sector.

This initiative is crucial for promoting sustainable development and addressing climate change challenges in Indonesia.

Green Building Certification (BGH PUPR) of the UGM Innovation and Creativity Center

This project focused on analyzing and modeling energy consumption for the UGM Innovation and Creativity Center to achieve green building certification.

We assessed static and dynamic thermal values to ensure compliance with green building standards.

The certification process involved rigorous evaluations of energy efficiency measures and sustainable practices implemented in the building.

This project highlights the importance of green building certifications in promoting sustainable architecture and construction practices.

Smart Windows: An Overview of Active and Passive Technologies for Energy-Efficient Buildings

This research provides an overview of smart window technologies, including active and passive systems designed for energy-efficient buildings.

We explored various technologies that allow windows to adapt to environmental conditions, enhancing energy performance and occupant comfort.

The findings emphasize the potential of smart windows to contribute to sustainable building designs and reduce energy consumption.

This project aims to inform architects and engineers about the latest advancements in window technologies for energy-efficient buildings.

Coiled-Up Silencer Tubes for Outdoor Noise Insulation Applications in Urban Buildings

This project addresses the growing concern of noise pollution in urban areas and its impact on health and well-being.

We developed coiled-up silencer tubes designed to effectively reduce outdoor noise levels in residential and commercial buildings.

The research involved testing various designs and materials to optimize sound insulation performance.

This project contributes to the development of innovative solutions for improving urban living conditions through effective noise control measures.

Implementation of Solar Street Lighting in an Indonesian Community

This project focused on the implementation of solar street lighting systems in a community in Indonesia.

We assessed the feasibility and benefits of using renewable energy sources to enhance public safety and reduce energy costs.

The project involved community engagement and education on the importance of renewable energy for sustainable development.

This initiative aims to promote the use of solar energy in urban planning and infrastructure development.

IoT-Based Relative Humidity and Room Temperature Monitoring Tool for Mushroom Cultivation House

This project developed an IoT-based monitoring tool for controlling relative humidity and room temperature in mushroom cultivation houses.

We aimed to optimize growing conditions to enhance yield and quality of mushrooms while minimizing resource consumption.

The tool provides real-time data and alerts, allowing farmers to make informed decisions for better crop management.

This project highlights the role of technology in modern agriculture and its potential to improve productivity and sustainability.

Exploring Environmental Literacy in Indonesia: Focusing on Energy and Green Building

This research explores the concept of environmental literacy in Indonesia, particularly in relation to energy and green building practices.

We assessed the current state of environmental education and its impact on sustainable development in the country.

The findings emphasize the need for increased awareness and education on environmental issues to foster a culture of sustainability.

This project aims to inform policymakers and educators about the importance of integrating environmental literacy into curricula and community programs.
